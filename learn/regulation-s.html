Regulation S

For the International, Non-U.S. Investor

The offshore lane of U.S. capital markets: a safe harbor that lets non-U.S. persons participate in American private credit — cleanly, compliantly, outside U.S. registration.

01

What Reg S Is

Regulation S is the SEC’s safe harbor for offers and sales made outside the United States. Two conditions anchor it: the transaction must be an “offshore transaction” — the buyer is outside the U.S. — and there may be no “directed selling efforts” into the U.S. market.

Securities sold under Reg S carry a distribution-compliance period restricting resale into the U.S., keeping the offshore and domestic pools distinct.
